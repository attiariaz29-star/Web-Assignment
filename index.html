Who We Are

Zaran Travel & Tours is a full service tour and travel agency offering thoughtfully planned travel experiences for individuals, couples, groups, and organizations. We specialize in local tours across Pakistan, including solo trips, honeymoon getaways, group tours, and corporate travel, all designed with comfort, care, and attention to detail.


We handle everything from transportation and hotel arrangements to complete tour planning, allowing our clients to travel with ease and confidence. Alongside local travel, we also provide rent a car services, private customized trips, international tours, and airline ticketing, making Zaran a reliable one stop solution for all travel needs.


At the heart of Zaran is a commitment to trust, honest service, and long term customer relationships. We focus on clear communication, dependable planning, and delivering experiences that our clients can rely on trip after trip.

About Us


Patron-in-Chief

Sardar Saeed Anwar


Sardar Saeed Anwar serves as the Patron-in-Chief of Zaran Travel & Tours, bringing with him decades of experience in local business leadership, public service, and community development. With a strong understanding of regional dynamics and an extensive network of professional relationships, he provides strategic guidance and oversight to the organization.

A seasoned politician and former District Mayor of Abbottabad, Sardar Saeed Anwar is widely recognized for his leadership, administrative insight, and commitment to structured growth. His experience in governance and public affairs plays a key role in strengthening Zaran's foundation as a trust-driven and professionally managed travel organization.

Under his patronage, Zaran Travel & Tours is guided by principles of integrity, reliability, and long-term relationship building, ensuring confidence and trust for clients and partners alike.
